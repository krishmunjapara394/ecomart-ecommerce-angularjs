FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your order, you can return it for a full refund. Items must be in original condition and packaging.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days. Express delivery is available for select locations and takes 2–3 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over ₹2,000.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! We offer professional assembly services for all furniture items. You can add assembly to your order during checkout, and our expert team will set everything up for you.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also view your order status anytime from your profile's order history section.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, UPI, and net banking. All payments are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways to ensure your data is safe.
